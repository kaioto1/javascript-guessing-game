Simple number guessing game

I have selected a random number between 1 and 1000. See if you can guess it in 10 turns or fewer.

How to play the game:

  1. Type your guess (number between 1 and 1000) into the field after "Enter a guess"
  2. Click on the button "Submit guess"
  3. I will tell you if your guess was too high or too low

It is possible to guess the correct number in 7 turns.

Good luck!
